BIG PROJECTS

Champion's Dispute (Board Game)

Champion's Dispute is a free for all battle royal game between 3-6 players. The objective is to emerge as the sole surviving player by defeating other players in combat. There are six unique champions (classes) that you will be able to choose from, each having their own strengths and weaknesses to play around.

Champion's Dispute is my largest project to date. I personally handled everything from the art and game design to the graphic design and component creation myself.

Adobe Illustrator DungeonDraft Krita
Team: Solo 3 Years

Chained 2 Violence

Chained 2 Violence is a two-player co-op arena game with roguelike elements. In this harrowing experience, players are enslaved and chained together, forced to battle relentless waves of cloned subjects created by the sinister ChronoDynamics corporation. Will you endure long enough to break free?

Chained 2 Violence originated as a group project in one of my game development courses. It is now being polished and prepared for a full release on Steam, and wishlisting is already available.

Unity C# Co-op Multiplayer Rougelike action
Team: 8 People 5 Months
